3. How to disable cookies by setting your browser

Cookies stored on your device are saved on your device for a certain period of time (you can delete them on your part). In most browsers, the acceptance of cookies is enabled from the initial setting, but you can disable cookies by changing the settings of your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may prevent the website from working properly. The procedure for changing cookie settings in general browsers is as follows.

  • Google Chrome
  • Apple Safari (macOS)
