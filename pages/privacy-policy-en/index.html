Privacy Policy

May 20, 2026

Ma Te Japan, Ltd. takes great care to maintain the confidentiality of personal information, and we believe that we can provide better services by having our customers understand our regulations on privacy protection.

1. Definition of Personal Information

"Personal information" in this policy means the following that can identify the individual customer individually or by combination.

  • A: Name
  • B: Date of birth
  • C: Address
  • D: Phone number
  • E: Fax number
  • F: E-mail address
  • G: Financial institution name, branch name, account number and name
  • H: Information by image, video, and audio

2. Basic Approach to the Handling of Personal Information

    1. Ma Te Japan and its employees (officers, employees, temporary employees, contract employees, etc., all persons engaged in our business, such as officers, employees, temporary employees, contract employees, etc.) shall strictly operate related laws and regulations such as the Personal Information Protection Law and this Privacy Policy, and strive to prevent leakage, falsification, loss, and unauthorized acquisition of information.
    1. We will manage personal information in an accurate and up-to-date state. However, in order to manage the usage history, we will keep past information.
    1. We will appropriately supervise our employees to ensure that personal information is managed securely. In addition, we regularly provide education and guidance to all employees in order to ensure the protection of personal information.
  • For inquiries and requests regarding personal information, please contact the reception desk inquiry@ma-te.jp

3. Means of personal information protection

    1. We do not outsource the total management of personal information.
    1. Information protection between the companies is based on the non-disclosure agreement between the partner companies, affiliates, and/or partner companies
    1. We implement and update your security best practices using modern cryptographic software.
    1. We do not keep paper based records of Personal Information.
    1. If Personal Information is faxed to us, we digitalize it and immediately destroy our copy.

4. Purpose of use of personal information

1. Guests

We collect and use your personal information in accordance with the following purposes for the purpose of providing and improving the provision and improvement of services and promoting appropriate sales promotion activities.

  • A: Provision of services and sales of products
  • B: Questions, answers, and notifications associated with service management
  • C: Transfer of monthly usage fee, etc. and transfer of agency margins, etc.
  • D: Questionnaire survey on satisfaction, policies, preferences, etc.
  • F: Sending various information by e-mail magazine, direct mail, facsimile, etc. and distributing advertisements
  • G: Management of response history and contents, improvement of service quality

2. Business Partners

We collect and use personal information held by partner companies, affiliates, and partner companies in line with the following purposes.

  • A: Performance of services
  • B: Questions, answers, and notifications associated with service management
  • C: Contract conclusion and business partner review
  • D: For statistical research ( After Data Anonymization )
  • E: Business incidental or security incident reports

3. Employees, etc.

We collect and use the personal information of our employees and applicants in accordance with the following purposes.

  • A: Recruitment selection and office communication
  • B: Management of personnel, health, security, etc
  • C: Social labor insurance procedures, tax collection and payment
  • D: Business incidental to and related to the above

The personal information of employees will be kept for the period during which it is required to be kept under the relevant laws and regulations, and after that, they will be destroyed sequentially. In addition, the resumes, etc. of those who have not decided to be hired will be immediately destroyed, and Ma Te Japan will not retain this information.

4. Common matters

When handling personal information other than the purpose of use of 1 to 3, we will obtain the consent of the person in advance.

5. Proper acquisition and minimum principles

  • Ma Te Japan acquires personal information lawfully and appropriately, and does not acquire it by false or other illegal means.
  • Ma Te Japan will not acquire any sensitive personal information that includes race, creed, medical history, etc. without obtaining the consent of the person in advance, except as required by law.
  • The personal information we acquire is "4. Purpose of use of personal information" and limited to the minimum necessary to operate Ma Te Japan.

6. Notes on The Browser

Access Logs

The information you access to our website is recorded as an access log. The access log includes the host name, IP address, browser used, OS type, access date and time, etc. of the access line owner, but does not include personal information.

Cookies

We use a technology called cookies for the purpose of improving The Service and smooth operation. You can use cookies to identify the device you are using, but we do not collect any personal information. However, when linking the access status of the website collected by cookies with personal information, the consent of the person in question shall be obtained. For details on the purpose of use of cookies, please refer to the Cookie Policy

TLS (Transport Layer Security)

We use the authentication and encryption method called TLS. TLS is a technology that verifies the existence of websites and prevents unauthorized access to information by encrypting communications between your device and servers.

    • A: When we have obtained the consent of the customer for disclosure and provision
    • B: When it is necessary to follow the warrant issued by the judge
